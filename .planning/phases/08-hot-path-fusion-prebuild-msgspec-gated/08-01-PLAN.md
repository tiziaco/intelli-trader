<objective>
Fuse the per-bar portfolio mark-to-market into a SINGLE pass over the open positions (Req 1, D-04).
Currently `position_manager.get_total_market_value` (:286-295) and `get_total_unrealized_pnl`
(:297-305) each iterate `_storage.get_positions()` independently every bar; a locked-margin basis
loop iterates positions a third time. D-04: `position_manager` grows a private single-pass valuation
producing total market value + total unrealised PnL + the locked-margin basis in ONE iteration; the
public accessors delegate to the fused result and keep returning byte-identical Decimals.

Purpose: Cut the ~12.5% per-bar mark-to-market cost (scales on the W2 symbol axis) with zero numeric
change. This is the ONLY cross-component change in the phase — `portfolio_handler`/`portfolio` ask
`position_manager` for the margin basis instead of iterating positions themselves.

Output: Fused valuation method, delegating accessors, a fusion-equivalence test, oracle byte-exact.
</objective>
