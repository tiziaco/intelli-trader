Ship the poll seam's Arm-A logic: a lean `UniverseSelectionModel` in `membership.py` (the D-20
growth target — a pure "what should the universe be?" selection seam, NOT a ranking engine) and a
new `UniverseHandler` (`universe/universe_handler.py`) that hosts `on_time` (poll → D-06 filter →
`universe.apply` → emit `UniverseUpdateEvent` only on a non-empty delta) and the ADD branch of
`on_universe_update` (warmup-BEFORE-subscribe for each added symbol).

This implements the selection/poll half of D-02 and the "screeners propose, membership disposes"
split (D-04): selection is pure and queue-free; the handler owns the cadence-driven poll + the
add-side side effects. The REMOVE branch, the flat-detect detach, and the admission gate are
plan 04; the live timer + composition wiring are plan 05. `UniverseHandler` keeps ZERO membership
duplication — it reads and mutates only through the injected `Universe` (the user's guiding
constraint).

Purpose: give plan 04 a handler to extend and plan 05 a poll+add surface to wire live.
Output: the selection model, `UniverseHandler` (on_time + add branch), unit tests.
