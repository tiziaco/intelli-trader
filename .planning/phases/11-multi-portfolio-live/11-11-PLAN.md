<objective>
Land the W7 test boundary (D-28) — the phase's own proof that multi-portfolio live actually works,
end to end, including the restart.

**The reason this is in this phase rather than the next one:** with no web layer in scope, the phase
that builds a surface must drive the real external path or it ships untested. That precedent is
established twice already in this milestone. The overlap with the later test-gates phase is the point:
this phase must not depend on a later phase to discover it is broken.

The path proven here is: durable definition rows -> **restart** -> rehydrate -> two portfolios trading
independently against their own accounts, with fills routed to the correct portfolio.

**Two paper accounts on the simulated venue is the right venue for it.** Two portfolios, two distinct
account ids on the paper venue, each with its own simulated account — which D-06's
every-portfolio-names-an-account rule makes a first-class configuration rather than a test hack.
Deterministic, CI-safe, no credentials. The OKX demo alternative was rejected concretely: the available
demo sub-account trades only two pairs under a regulatory whitelist, both are pre-seeded non-flat, and
sells are blocked by a price floor above the best bid, so an online settlement end-to-end cannot reach
a fill there at all.

plugin the already-built SHARED simulated exchange, so two paper accounts necessarily resolve to one
exchange object. This test cannot gate per-account exchange routing, and must not pretend to — that
gate lives in plan 11-06 with a fake multi-account plugin. A routing assertion here would pass while
proving nothing, which is worse than no test.

Output: one integration test covering independent trading, correct fill attribution with the negative
asserted, and a restart with stable ids — plus the phase validation document closed out.
