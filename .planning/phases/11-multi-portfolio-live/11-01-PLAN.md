<objective>
Land the W1 schema half of Phase 11 (D-28 wave W1): the two missing durable definition tables and
their stores. `venue_accounts` gives an account the flat, one-row-per-account home the owner asked for
(D-05), keyed by the `(venue_name, account_id)` PAIR that D-01 makes the identity of an account.
`portfolios` gives portfolios the definition row they have never had (D-08) — the finding that
motivated this phase: seven portfolio-scoped child tables all record what a portfolio HAS, none record
what it IS.

The B2 todo (`strategy_portfolio_subscriptions.portfolio_id` -> `Uuid` with an `ON DELETE CASCADE`
FK) is NOT in this plan — it moved to 11-03, where it lands with the Revision 2 migration that D-29
binds it to. This plan only creates the `portfolios` table that B2 will later reference.

Purpose: every later wave (credentials, account minting, rehydrate, reconciliation) reads or writes
these two tables. Nothing else in the phase can start without them.

Output: two new schema-pure stores on the shared `SqlEngine` spine, their registrars, and unit tests
proving the D-14 unique constraint and the D-06 paper row.

**Scope boundary (D-28 W1):** tables and stores ONLY. Alembic revisions AND the B2 fold-in are plan
11-03. No live wiring, no `build_live_system` edits, and no edit to
`itrader/storage/strategy_registry_store.py`.
</objective>
