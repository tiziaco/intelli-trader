<objective>
Deliver the D-03 rider: collapse the redundant `signal_store` surfacing so the store is
reached through its owning handler directly, matching the delegation pattern every other
persistence store already follows (order storage via `order_handler.storage`, etc.).

The `SignalStore` is owned and constructed in-module by `StrategiesHandler` (CTX-02, upheld).
Today that same instance is redundantly re-surfaced on two composition holders â€” the `Engine`
dataclass field and the `BacktestTradingSystem._signal_store` ctor-param/attribute â€” and the
public accessors read the holder copy. This plan removes the redundant plumbing and repoints
the accessors to read `self.engine.strategies_handler.signal_store` directly.

Purpose: eliminate a duplicated surface that duplicates the handler-owned store; align the
facade with the established store-access convention. Behavior-preserving â€” the accessors return
the identical store instance, so the backtest oracle stays byte-exact.

Output: a leaner `Engine` holder (no `signal_store` field) and a leaner
`BacktestTradingSystem` (no `signal_store` param, no `_signal_store` attribute) with accessors
that read through the handler.

Boundary: this is a DISTINCT cleanup from the CTX-04 rename (03-01). Per D-03 it is planned as
its own step and must NOT introduce a `signal_store` `@property`.
</objective>
