<objective>
Deliver CTX-04: rename the shared SQL spine class `SqlBackend` to `SqlEngine`, move its
module `itrader/storage/backend.py` to `itrader/storage/engine.py`, tighten
`EngineContext.sql_engine` from `Optional[Any]` to the concrete `Optional[SqlEngine]`,
and update every importer/reference (~118 refs across ~34 files). Under D-01 (full
consistency sweep) the vocabulary is unified end-to-end: the enumerated handler/factory
`backend=` params become `sql_engine=` and the `_backend` fields become `_sql_engine`.
Under D-02 the old `SqlBackend` name is deleted entirely — NO deprecation alias.

Purpose: a clean, self-consistent SQL-spine vocabulary with zero dead names, verified by
`mypy --strict` (the completeness net for itrader/) and grep (the completeness net for
tests/, which mypy does not typecheck). Behavior-preserving — the backtest oracle stays
byte-exact and the OKX import-inertness gate stays green.

Output: the renamed `SqlEngine` class + `itrader/storage/engine.py` module, the tightened
`EngineContext.sql_engine: Optional[SqlEngine]` annotation, the `sql_engine`/`_sql_engine`
param/field vocabulary across the enumerated scope, and all importers repointed.

This plan is CTX-04 only. The redundant `signal_store` surface collapse (decision D-03) is
a SEPARATE, wave-2 plan (03-02) and is NOT part of this rename diff.
</objective>
