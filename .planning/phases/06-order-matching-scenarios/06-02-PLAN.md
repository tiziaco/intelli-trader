<!-- Engine fill formulas (matching_engine._evaluate) — author bars so each fill price is round. -->
BUY LIMIT  (limit entry): open <= trigger -> fill at OPEN (gap-through, better);   else low <= trigger -> fill at TRIGGER (in-bar touch)
SELL STOP  (stop, e.g. SELL entry): low  <= trigger -> fill at MIN(open, trigger)  (pessimistic gap-down)
BUY  STOP  (stop entry):           high >= trigger -> fill at MAX(open, trigger)  (pessimistic gap-up)
MARKET     (any):                  always next bar -> fill at OPEN

Entry-price provenance (D-02): a LIMIT/STOP entry rests at the DECISION-bar close
(signal_event.price = to_money(decision-bar close)). Set that close to the desired trigger,
then author the FOLLOWING bars to produce touch / gap-through / gap-down / gap-up.
