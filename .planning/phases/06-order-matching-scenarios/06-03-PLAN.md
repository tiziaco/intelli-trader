<!-- Bracket engine facts (matching_engine two-pass on_bar + _pick_bracket_winner). -->
Pass 1: parent (parent_order_id is None) fills independently and leaves the book.
Pass 2: children whose parent NO LONGER rests arm; a child whose parent still rests is DORMANT (CR-01).
A parent filling THIS bar unlocks its children against the SAME bar's high/low.
Per bracket, at most ONE child fills per bar; if BOTH STOP(SL) and LIMIT(TP) are candidates, STOP wins.
When a leg fills, all siblings of that bracket are OCO-cancelled (CancelDecision -> FillEvent(CANCELLED)).
