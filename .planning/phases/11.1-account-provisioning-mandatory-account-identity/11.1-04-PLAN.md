<objective>
Land **D-07 (VENUE-06)**: `rng` joins `EngineContext`.

This is a consequence of D-06, landed ahead of it and alone. Today `ExecutionHandler` derives the
determinism RNG itself (`execution_handler.py:110-111`, reading `config.rng_seed` through
`_resolve_rng_seed`). From plan 11.1-07 the `SimulatedExchange` is built by `PaperVenuePlugin`, which
has no access to that private attribute — so unless the shared RNG rides on the mode-injection seam
first, the plugin either re-derives one (two instances, non-reproducible) or takes `None` (a fresh
unseeded `random.Random()`, per `simulated.py:67-71`). Either outcome moves the oracle, and neither
would be obvious from a diff.

Purpose: put the single seeded RNG where every downstream builder can reach it, WITHOUT changing its
seed, its identity, or the number of instances.

Output: a required `rng` field on `EngineContext`; four updated construction sites; an
`ExecutionHandler` that receives rather than derives; an identity test that no seed-equality check
could substitute for.
</objective>
