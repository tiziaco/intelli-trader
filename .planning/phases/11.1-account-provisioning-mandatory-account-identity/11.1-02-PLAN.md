## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| signal -> admission | An externally-originated `SIGNAL` (D-10 live ingress) reaches `OrderHandler.on_signal` -> `AdmissionManager`. The venue allowlist and the cash reservation are the two admission-side controls on that path. |
| admission -> cash ledger | The reservation amount computed here is what is locked against the portfolio's available cash. Under-computing it lets an order through with insufficient headroom. |
