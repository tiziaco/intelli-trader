<objective>
Land **D-02 + D-03 (VENUE-02)**: `Portfolio` receives a built `Account` and mints none, and
`VenuePlugin.new_account` becomes the sole account factory.

This is the phase's governing principle applied at its original site â€” *objects receive their
collaborators; composition constructs them.* `Portfolio` currently selects and builds its own account
kind (`portfolio.py:176-180`), duplicating a branch that already lives in
`PaperVenuePlugin.new_account`, whose docstring openly admits it is the older implementation copied
verbatim. Two copies of one selection rule is the defect the phase exists to remove.

It lands LAST among the structural changes because it needs everything before it: D-01 (plan 11.1-03)
removed the back-reference that made a pre-built account impossible, and D-08's `VenueBundles` (plans
11.1-05 / 11.1-07) is what gives `PortfolioHandler` something to ask.

**RESEARCH F-1 is settled here, not discovered by the executor.** D-03's premise â€” that `new_account`
can simply drop `portfolio_ref` because D-01 removed the need â€” is false for `OkxVenuePlugin`, which
uses that parameter for D-11 venue-account IDENTITY resolution. The decision stands; the replacement
path is specified below.

Purpose: one owner for account-kind selection, and a domain object that no longer wires itself.

Output: a `Portfolio` that takes its account; a `PortfolioHandler` that builds it through the venue
plugin; a `new_account` with no `portfolio_ref` and an intact D-11 guard; ~56 updated test
construction sites.
</objective>
