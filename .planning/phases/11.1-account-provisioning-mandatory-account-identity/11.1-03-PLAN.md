<objective>
Land **D-01 (VENUE-01)**: the `Account` leaf stops carrying a reference back to the `Portfolio` that
owns it. The mutual reference is an accident ‚Äî `SimulatedCashAccount` stores `self.portfolio` and
never reads it (and that is the byte-exact oracle leaf), while only `SimulatedMarginAccount` reads it,
in exactly two methods. Those reads become method arguments supplied by `PortfolioHandler`, which is
already their only production caller.

This plan lands **alone and first** among the domain changes because it is genuinely independent:
`Portfolio` keeps minting its own leaf here (just without passing `self`), so nothing needs a
`VenueBundles` yet. D-02 ‚Äî `Portfolio` receiving a *built* account ‚Äî needs the venue path in place and
lands in plan 11.1-09.

Purpose: make D-02/D-03 possible. `Portfolio` cannot receive a pre-built `Account` while the `Account`
constructor demands the `Portfolio` that does not exist yet.

Output: two portfolio-free simulated account constructors; two re-signed margin methods; two
`PortfolioHandler` pass-throughs that now supply the inputs; three updated test files.
</objective>
