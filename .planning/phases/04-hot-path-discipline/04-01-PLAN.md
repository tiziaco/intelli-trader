<objective>
Realize the PERF-03 hot-loop logging win with a behavior-preserving set of changes: a central
level-gate in the `ITraderStructLogger` wrapper (D-02) that short-circuits below-level calls before
the 9-processor structlog pipeline; demotion of the per-bar admission-rejection log from `error` to
`warning` with a cached `isEnabledFor(WARNING)` guard (D-01); a new `ITRADER_DISABLE_LOGS` kill-switch
(D-08); a curated, owner-signed-off removal of redundant hot-path `debug()` calls (D-04); and a
gate-transparency drift-lock test + written audit (D-06). The admission eager list-comp arg is left
as-is by decision (D-03).

Purpose: hotspot #4 (~6% W1 / ~22% W2) is the structlog pipeline running on every below-level call;
the central gate is the whole logging win, the demotion gates the per-bar admission log out of the
ERROR benchmark level. This is behavior-only — it changes log VOLUME, never emitted CONTENT at an
enabled level, so the byte-exact SMA_MACD oracle is unaffected.

Output: gated logger, demoted admission log, kill-switch env var, curated debug deletes, and the
logging drift-lock test.
</objective>
