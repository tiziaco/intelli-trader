by the starred-header phase-lookup bug, so the signal is recorded here manually.)

**The noun now primary:** **`strategy_name`** тАФ the per-instance durable identity (D-02) that every
one of the nine verbs addresses. The event's anchor moves from `symbol` (a ticker-verb detail
belonging to exactly two of nine verbs) to `strategy_name` (the instance identity common to all of
them).

**Decision: `promote`.** `strategy_name` is promoted to the event's sole required identity anchor;
`symbol` is demoted from a required field to a per-variant detail of the `add_ticker`/`remove_ticker`
factories (`symbol: str | None = None`).

**Rationale:** six of the nine D-09 verbs (`add`, `remove`, `enable`, `disable`, `reconfigure`,
`subscribe_portfolio`, `unsubscribe_portfolio`) carry no symbol at all, so a required `symbol` would
force every one of them to pass a meaningless sentinel. Promoting the general identity and demoting
the specific detail is the correct move here rather than add-alongside: D-08 already explicitly
rejected separate typed events per command family, and `strategy_name` is already the durable
identity D-02 locked. The two assuming call sites тАФ `__str__` and the `symbol = event.symbol` read at
`universe_handler.py:498` тАФ are updated in Task 1/Task 2 rather than worked around.

**Non-blocking:** this is recorded, not gated on.
</assumption_delta_decision>
