<objective>
Land the D-06 data model: add `strategy_type` to `strategy_registry`, create the
`strategy_portfolio_subscriptions` child table, and drop the redundant P4 `strategy_subscriptions`
(venue, symbol, timeframe) table — in **both** the registrar and a new Alembic migration chained onto
the measured head `system_stats`.

Purpose: rehydrate (D-01) cannot run until the store can answer "which instances, of which type, with
which config, subscribed to which portfolios". The P4 store has the shape for three of those four and
carries a fourth table that models the wrong edge.

Output: a schema-pure store with portfolio-subscription CRUD + a replay-safe, non-destructive
migration.

Decisions implemented: **D-06** (two tables, `enabled` as its own column, drop `strategy_subscriptions`,
`tickers` stay in `config_json`), **D-18** (keep the `strategy_registry` / `StrategyRegistryStore`
names), **D-02** (the `strategy_name` natural PK is unchanged; no second durable id).
</objective>
