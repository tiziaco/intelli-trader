# Phase 10.1 Plan 01: Constructor-Own the Three Live Deps — Summary

DECOMP-01a is satisfied: `registry_store`, `strategy_catalog`, and `portfolio_read_model`
are all real at `StrategiesHandler.__init__` on both composition roots, with zero
post-construction dependency assignment left anywhere in `itrader/`.

## What was built

**Task 1 — `StrategyRegistryStorageFactory`** (`itrader/strategy_handler/storage/registry_storage_factory.py`,
4-space, commits `46a96d47` RED / `9720fe72` GREEN). Environment-keyed construction mirroring
`SignalStorageFactory`: `backtest`/`test` → `None`; `live` without a spine → `None`; `live` with a
spine → `StrategyRegistryStore`, gated behind the D-21 `has_table("strategy_registry")` probe
relocated out of `live_trading_system.py` along with its WARNING text and its full rationale
comment. SQLAlchemy and the store import stay lazy inside the `'live'` arm (GATE-01). Unknown
environment raises `ConfigurationError`. 7 unit tests drive a real in-memory SQLite spine, so the
`has_table` probe is genuinely exercised without monkeypatching and without Postgres.

**Task 2 — handler owns `registry_store`** (`strategies_handler.py`, TABS, commit `642509dc`).
Derived in `__init__` from `(environment, sql_engine)`, mirroring the `signal_store` idiom, using an
explicit `is not None` override test rather than `or`. The `set_universe` docstring was corrected:
RESEARCH D-2's finding is confirmed in the live code — `universe_wiring.py:100/109` calls it on the
backtest path — so the docstring now states the oracle is inert *by construction*
(`Readiness.READY` + ticker-derived membership), not by the universe being absent.

**Task 3 — compose threading + deletions** (commits `aa8e835b` RED / `f8a2d065` GREEN).
`compose_engine` gained a `strategy_catalog: Optional[Any]` kwarg with a NumPy-style Parameters
entry; `StrategiesHandler` now receives `strategy_catalog` and `portfolio_read_model=portfolio_handler`
at construction. The three assignments at `live_trading_system.py:1630/1641/1642`, the `has_table`
probe, and the now-orphaned `_sa_inspect` / `StrategyRegistryStore` imports are deleted;
`rehydrate_strategies` stays in place, gated on the handler-owned store. The D-09 ordering rationale
was migrated rather than dropped, restated as now satisfied structurally.
