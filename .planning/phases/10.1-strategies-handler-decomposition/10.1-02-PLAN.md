<objective>
DECOMP-01 (part 1 of 2) — extract the roster into `ManagedStrategies`: the single owner of
`strategies`, `min_timeframe`, `_pending_removals`, and the SHORT-01/D-07 registration rules.
The handler keeps its public surface by delegation.

Purpose: the roster is shared state between the data plane (`on_bar` iterates it) and the control
plane (every verb mutates it). Giving it one owner is what lets 10.1-03 move the control plane out
without the two planes reaching into each other's attributes.

Output: `itrader/strategy_handler/managed_strategies.py`, delegating handler accessors, and an
isolation test suite that exercises the roster rules without a handler or a queue.
</objective>
