<objective>
Resolve the D-12 code-vs-decision gap that gates the entire feed, and stand up the offline
test infrastructure the LiveBarFeed unit matrix consumes.

Phase-3's `LiveBarFeed.update(closed_bar)` routes each bar to a per-`(symbol, timeframe)` ring
and monotonic guard — but the Phase-2 `ClosedBar` TypedDict carries only `ts,open,high,low,close,
volume` (verified in RESEARCH Pitfall 3). Without the routing keys in the payload, `update()`
cannot self-route (D-01's "the dict already carries the routing keys" is false against the code).
D-12 co-shapes the Phase-2 seam: extend `ClosedBar` with `symbol` + `timeframe` and populate them
at the provider edge, on BOTH the live-stream and REST-backfill paths.

Purpose: unblock the feed's routing (FEED-01 ring keying) and its warmup source (FEED-03), and
give every downstream feed test a deterministic, socket-free `ClosedBar` source.
Output: an extended `ClosedBar` + populated provider + shared `tests/unit/price/conftest.py`
fixtures.
</objective>
