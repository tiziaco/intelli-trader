<objective>
Fold the scattered live-only module constants into pure domain config models and rewire their direct
readers (CFG-03, D-08).

Per D-08 this plan DEFINES the new config blocks AND rewires the direct constant readers now; the shared
`StreamSupervisor` consolidation that will eventually consume them is deferred to P5. The `_STREAM_RECONNECT_*`
family (triplicated across three files with identical numbers) and the OKX stream symbol/timeframe hardcodes
fold into a new `StreamSettings`; `_WARMUP_MARGIN` and `_BACKFILL_PAGE` fold into a new `FeedProviderSettings`;
the `_OKX_*`/`_PAPER_*` constants in the live composition root are retired (the `_PAPER_*` aliases dereference
to the retained `PAPER_PARITY_*` parity anchor — Pitfall 4 forbids any value drift). The `_OKX_INTERVALS`
timeframe→token lookup table is explicitly NOT folded (functional data, not a tunable). All eight fold sites
are live-only (none on the backtest hot path), so the oracle risk is low — the real gate to protect is
inertness (Pitfall 1: the new config modules must import nothing live).

Purpose: give every live-supervisor/feed knob a single typed config home, removing the triplicated magic
numbers so P5's shared supervisor has one source to read.
Output: `config/stream.py`, its unit test, barrel re-exports, and the rewired readers — grep-clean.
</objective>
