<objective>
Give the `(venue_name, account_id)` pair exactly one storage site on `Portfolio`, and move the
definition-to-runtime-graph mapping out of `PortfolioHandler` into a composition-owned
`PortfolioFactory`.

Purpose: `PortfolioHandler` currently holds `VenueBundles` and builds account leaves itself — three
uses of `self._venue_bundles`, all inside `_new_compute_account`, guarded by an `is None` refusal
that exists only because the collaborator is `Optional`. The owner rejected three prior framings of
this as *"moving the mess around"*: correcting the key `_new_compute_account` passes, having the
caller pass a finished `Account`, and having the caller pass a pair-bound factory. The answer is that
the handler should not be building account leaves at all — composition constructs collaborators,
objects receive them.

This plan does the **structural half** (D-23, D-24). Plan 09 does the **behavioural half** (D-25's
`create`/`materialize` split, ACCT-03/05/07 and the WR-11 fixture).

Output: `itrader/portfolio_handler/portfolio_factory.py`, a `Portfolio` whose pair has one home, and
a handler with no `VenueBundles`.
