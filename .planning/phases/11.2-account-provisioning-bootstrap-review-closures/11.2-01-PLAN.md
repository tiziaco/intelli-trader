<objective>
Make `state_storage` a required keyword on the simulated account leaves and on the paper
`account_factory` closure, deleting the `PortfolioStateStorageFactory.create("backtest")` fallback and
the `getattr(...)` defaults that silently substitute a float for missing cash.

Purpose: this is D-39's W1 foundation and the phase's **binding ordering constraint** â€” ACCT-05 adds
portfolio-creation paths, and landing the required kwarg first means those new paths structurally
cannot forget the persistence seam. Landing it after means adding paths through the trap and closing
it behind them; every such path yields a portfolio that reports itself live while persisting nothing,
behind a fully green backtest.

Output: `simulated.py` and `paper_plugin.py` with no defaulted persistence seam, plus a
`TypeError`-on-omission regression test.
