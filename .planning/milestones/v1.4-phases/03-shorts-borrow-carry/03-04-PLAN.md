<objective>
Close the v1.0 M5b CR-01 cover-arm hole: in `AdmissionManager._resolve_signal_quantity`,
generalize the long-only exit predicate to a **side-agnostic exit** so a BUY-to-cover on an
open short routes through the proven `resolve_exit` (SHORT-02 / D-05), and an over-cover
**clamps to flat** — the excess does not auto-open a long (D-06). Confirm short PnL is already
first-class (SHORT-03 / D-08 — the `PositionSide.SHORT` branches exist; add tests only). Fold
in WR-04: floor `_effective_leverage` at `Decimal("1")` with a zero/sub-1 cap guard (D-09).

Purpose: A cover BUY on a `SHORT_ONLY`/`LONG_SHORT` book currently fails the
`SELL and net_quantity > 0` predicate (`admission_manager.py:703`) and falls into the
entry-sizing arm (:726), flipping the book long. The fix is ONE generalized branch (not a
second near-duplicate — RESEARCH anti-pattern): detect "order action opposes the open
position's side" and pass `abs(net_quantity)` to `resolve_exit`, which already operates on a
magnitude and is a no-op at `exit_fraction == 1`. The long path stays byte-exact (`abs` is
identity for `net>0`, A2). Clamp-to-flat is implicit: `resolve_exit` returns at most the full
magnitude. The Phase-2 over-close guard (`portfolio.py:399-404`) stays as defense-in-depth.
Output: a fixed cover-arm + WR-04 floor + SHORT-03 confirmation tests.
</objective>
