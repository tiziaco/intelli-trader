<objective>
Close CR-01 (verification blocker) per **D-24**: make `VenueAccount.drop_pending` (`account/venue.py:702`) a **no-op on the single-channel spot leaf** so the `_pending` overlay is held until terminal release, instead of being dropped on ack.

Purpose: the actually-wired venue is constructed `market_type='spot'` (`live_trading_system.py:471`). Its `_write_balance_stream` (`venue.py:281-314`) is positions-only — it NEVER refreshes `_venue_balance`, which is re-baselined solely by `snapshot()` (D-01 single-channel cash). `available_balance` = `_venue_balance + _ledger_delta - _pending_total()` (`venue.py:557`). So on spot the `_pending` overlay is the SOLE tracker of a resting order's cash hold for its entire life; the current unconditional `drop_pending` pops it on ack and snaps `available_balance` back to the full settled balance while the order is still open on the venue — a live buying-power OVER-statement that admits a second order against already-committed cash. On spot there was never a double-count for D-15 to remove; the drop-on-ack stays dormant-valid for a future margin/swap account whose cash channel refreshes `_venue_balance`.
Output: `drop_pending` guarded; new RED→GREEN spot-premise test; oracle byte-exact; mypy strict clean.
</objective>
