<objective>
Split today's synchronous `warmup()` (REST fetch → per-bar `update()` → `_emit` flood) into the two
non-blocking halves the WR-02 pipeline needs (D-03): (1) a NEW non-emitting `LiveBarFeed.absorb_warmup`
that reuses the exact `_deliver` ring / `_last_delivered` (L) / `_newest_bars` logic MINUS `_emit` (the
"silent ring absorb" that keeps the feed read-model warm and L-continuous without putting a tradeable
`BarEvent` on the queue — RESEARCH OQ1), and (2) a NEW async `OkxDataProvider.spawn_warmup` that runs
ONLY the REST fetch on the connector loop (no engine state) and hands ALL fetched bars back to the engine
thread via ONE `BarsLoaded` event (bulk transport, D-03/D-03a), emitting `BarsLoadFailed` with a scrubbed
reason on failure (D-04).

Purpose: the fetch/absorb mechanics the UniverseHandler warmup consumers (Plan 06) and the strategy warm
(Plan 04) build on. Cold-feed-ring is a real correctness gap (the documented warmup-before-subscribe L
contract) — the silent absorb closes it.
Output: absorb + spawn_warmup primitives, live-only, oracle-inert (never on the backtest path).
</objective>
