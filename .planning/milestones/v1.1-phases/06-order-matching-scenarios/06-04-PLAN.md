<!-- Gap fill formulas (matching_engine._evaluate). A "clean-through" gap jumps the open clean past the trigger. -->
STOP gap (pessimistic): SELL STOP low<=trigger -> MIN(open,trigger); BUY STOP high>=trigger -> MAX(open,trigger). A clean gap makes open the fill (worse than the trigger).
LIMIT gap (better):     BUY LIMIT open<=trigger -> OPEN; SELL LIMIT open>=trigger -> OPEN. A clean gap makes open the fill (better than the trigger).
gap-past-both-legs: a bracket bar whose gap clears BOTH SL(stop) and TP(limit). Both are candidates -> _pick_bracket_winner picks STOP -> STOP FILLED, TP OCO-CANCELLED (exactly one leg fills).
