# Phase 260718-e36 Plan 01: Fix Phase 10 re-review WR-01 (rehydrate quarantine) + deactivated-skip Summary

WR-01 re-review closed: a stored strategy row whose timeframe is finer-than-base (or a
non-whole-multiple) is now quarantined per-instance at rehydrate (D-19: skip + one CRITICAL
alert + continue, row never mutated) instead of raising `UnwarmableTimeframeError` out of
`register_strategy_warmup` and crashing the entire live boot; and `derive_warmup_depth` /
`register_strategy_warmup` now skip `is_active == False` strategies so a dark row neither
inflates the ring depth nor (when finer-than-base) raises from the ladder ŌĆö with the
`NEWEST_BAR_ONLY` floor preserved for a post-filter-empty roster in both branches.

### Task 2 ŌĆö skip is_active==False strategies in the warmup ladder (STRAT-03)
