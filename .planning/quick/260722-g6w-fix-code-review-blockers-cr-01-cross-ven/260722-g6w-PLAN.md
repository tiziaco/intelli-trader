**CR-01** — the live composition root derives its account set and attaches venue accounts by
bare `account_id` STRING, discarding the venue half of the `(venue_name, account_id)` pair the
rest of the phase pins identity on. A durable portfolio on venue X is handed venue Y's

**CR-05** — `OkxConnectorPlugin.build` feeds a possibly-PARTIAL resolved credential mapping into
`OkxSettings`, a `pydantic_settings.BaseSettings`, so every credential field the per-account
prefix does not supply is silently completed from the ambient global `OKX_API_*` set.

Purpose: both defects are silent cross-account/cross-venue conflations that pass a fully green
suite today. CR-01 makes `ReconciliationCoordinator` snapshot one venue's account against another
venue's positions and lets `VenueReconciler` emit reconciling fills into the wrong portfolio.
CR-05 is the exact credential bleed `EnvCredentialResolver`'s fail-loud contract (T-11-18) exists
to prevent, reintroduced one layer down at FIELD granularity instead of REFERENCE granularity —
and the D-04 UID guard does not catch it, because the ambient secret belongs to a real account
whose UID is stable and gets trust-on-first-use recorded as this account's.
